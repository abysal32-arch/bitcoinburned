What to do with this

  1. Open a PSBT-capable wallet you already trust (Sparrow, Electrum, or your hardware wallet's own app).
  2. Import this PSBT and check every field against what you entered above — especially the burn amount.
  3. Sign it there. This page never sees your private key and cannot sign anything itself.
  4. Broadcast the signed transaction from that wallet, or via any node/explorer that accepts raw transactions.
